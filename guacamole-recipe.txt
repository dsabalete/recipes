Ingredients:
 - 2 advocados
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 lemon
 - 3 drops tabasco sauce
 - 1 spoon of coliander
 - 1 bag of 'nachos'

Directions:
 1. Peel and remove the advocados bones and put in the kitchen-machine.
 
 2. Squeeze the lemon over the advocado in order to prevent its oxidation.
 
 3. Ground the coriander and filter over the mix.
 
 4. Add 3 drops of tabasko sauce.
 
 5. Add a teaspoon of salt.
 
 6. Mix 20 seconds in the kitchen robot.

 7. Serve with nachos and enjoy!
